New Community Center Opens

Date: October 10, 2023

We are thrilled to announce the opening of our new community center, which will serve as a hub for activities, events, and gatherings for all members of our community. This state-of-the-art facility is equipped with modern amenities and spaces designed to accommodate a wide range of uses, from small meetings to large celebrations. We invite everyone to come and explore the new center and see all that it has to offer.
