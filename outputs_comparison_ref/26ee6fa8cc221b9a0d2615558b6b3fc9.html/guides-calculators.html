Frequently Asked Questions

What is car insurance?

Car insurance provides financial protection against physical damage or bodily injury resulting from traffic collisions.

How does home insurance work?

Home insurance covers losses and damages to an individual's house and to assets in the home.

Why do I need travel insurance?

Travel insurance protects you from unforeseen events such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, and more during your travels.

What does pet insurance cover?

Pet insurance covers veterinary expenses for illness or injury, and some policies include preventative care.

Glossary of Insurance Terms

Premium

A premium is the amount paid periodically to the insurer by the insured for covering risk.

Deductible

A deductible is the amount you pay out of pocket before your insurance covers the remaining costs.

Coverage

Coverage refers to the amount of protection you have through your insurance policy.
