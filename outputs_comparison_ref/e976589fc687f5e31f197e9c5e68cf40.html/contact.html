Frequently Asked Questions

What are the theatre's opening hours?

The theatre is open from 10 AM to 10 PM, Monday to Sunday.

How can I book tickets for performances?

Tickets can be booked online via our Book Tickets page.

Is there parking available?

Yes, there is a parking lot adjacent to the theatre.

Are refreshments available at the theatre?

Yes, we offer a variety of snacks and beverages at our concession stand.

Can I volunteer at the theatre?

Yes, we welcome volunteers. Please contact us for more information.
